The Core of Fairness: Random Number Generators (RNGs)

At the heart of every online casino game is a Random Number Generator (RNG). This technology ensures that outcomes—whether it’s the spin of a slot machine or the deal of a card—are completely random and unbiased. RNGs use sophisticated algorithms to generate sequences of numbers that determine game results. These systems are rigorously tested by independent organizations to ensure they meet strict standards.
